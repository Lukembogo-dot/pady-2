============================================
  PADY BACKUP TOOL
============================================

TO START A BACKUP:

  Double-click  "Start Backup.exe"

That's it. It will:
  1. Scan your Documents, Pictures, Desktop, and Downloads
  2. Let you choose what to back up and any filters
     (it remembers your choices from last time - press
     Enter to reuse them, or type new values)
  3. Show you the estimated size and time, and ask to confirm
  4. Save a single .zip file onto this drive

Nothing is copied or deleted until you confirm with "Y".
